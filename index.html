How to Play

Goal: Fly through rings to score points before time runs out or your HP hits zero!

  • Mouse: Control Pitch (Up/Down) and Yaw (Left/Right)
  • A / Left Arrow: Roll Left
  • D / Right Arrow: Roll Right
  • W / Up Arrow: Increase Speed
  • S / Down Arrow: Decrease Speed

Rings: Give points (smaller rings = more points!).

Obstacles (Grey Shapes): Reduce HP and Score!
